As a side project, I am currently working on implementing my own graphics engine using C++, OpenGL, and GLFW as window manager. During my free time I have been learning the graphics pipeline of OpenGL, vertex and index buffers, textures, shaders, translations, projection matrices and lighting. I plan to use this engine as an opportunity to learn and prototype as well as have some fun.
